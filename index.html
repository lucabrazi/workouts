Monday — Bodyweight + Jump Rope Circuit

Goal: Conditioning, pushups, legs, core, and posterior-chain balance.
Equipment: Jump rope

Warm-up

  • Marching knee lifts — 20 total
  • Arm circles — 10 forward, 10 backward
  • Bodyweight squats — 10
  • Hip hinges — 10
  • Easy jump rope — 30–60 skips

Main Circuit 3 Rounds

  • Alt, Double then Triple Hops
  • Pushups — 14
  • Jump rope — 60 skips
  • Lunges, varied types — 12 total
  • Jump rope — 60 skips
  • Heel touches / crunches — 15
  • Jump rope — 60 skips
  • Hip hinges, glute bridges, or good mornings — 10–12
  • Jump rope — 60 skips

Tuesday — Rest / Easy Movement

🧘‍♂️ Take it easy today!

  • Easy walk — optional
  • Light mobility — optional
  • Full rest — also fine

Wednesday — Full Body + Pulling + Curls

Goal: Full-body strength, pulling/posture balance, coordination, core control, and conditioning.
Equipment: Pull up bar (if available), Dumbbells for curls, Optional Weight for Turkish Getups

Warm-up 2 Rounds

  • Marching knee lifts — 20 total
  • Jumping jacks or step jacks — 20
  • Bodyweight squats — 10
  • Arm circles — 10 forward, 10 backward
  • Sledgehammer halos — 10 total
  • Hip hinges — 10

Main Circuit 4 Rounds

  • Y-T-W raises — 3 each position (5 if no pullups)
  • Shoulder-blade squeezes — 8–12
  • Pull-ups — 2 reps (Add a 3rd rep only if the first 2 feel easy)
  • Burpees — 2 reps (Step back, step forward, stand tall. Jump optional)
  • Standing cross-body punches — 30 total
  • Turkish get-ups — 2 total (1 per side, bodyweight or light weight)
  • Low-impact skaters — 20 total
  • Mountain climbers — 20 total (Slow and controlled)

Curl Finisher

  • Curls — 2–3 sets of 8–12 reps (Keep reps clean, stop with 1–2 in the tank)

Thursday — Rest / Recovery

🛌 Rest up. Avoid turning Thursday into a hard workout, because Friday is rope-focused.

  • Easy walk — optional
  • Light stretching — optional
  • Full rest — good choice

Friday — Jump Rope Conditioning

Goal: Rope rhythm, conditioning, footwork, and lower-impact endurance.
Equipment: Jump rope

Phase 1 — Warm-Up

  • Jogging in place — 60 total (30 per leg)
  • Forward and backward arm circles — 20 total
  • Jumping jacks — 40
  • Slow freestyle jump rope — 100

Phase 2 — Main Jump Rope Circuit 5 Rounds

  • Basic bounce — 60
  • Slow march in place — 20
  • Alternating skip — 60
  • Slow march in place — 20
  • Basic bounce — 60
  • Slow march in place — 20
  • Double hops — 30 total
  • Slow march in place — 20

Phase 3 — Cool-Down

  • Very slow jump rope — 50 reps
  • Static calf stretch against a wall — 3 holds per leg (5 deep breaths each)
  • Overhead tricep and shoulder stretch — 2 holds per arm (5 deep breaths each)

Flexible Stretching / Gap-Filler Day

Schedule: Fit this in wherever it feels best: Saturday, Sunday, or a lighter weekday. (Maintenance focus)

Gentle Warm-Up

  • Marching knee lifts — 40 total
  • Arm circles — 10 forward, 10 backward
  • Hip hinges — 10
  • Bodyweight squats — 10
  • Shoulder rolls — 10 forward, 10 backward

Mobility Circuit 2 Rounds

  • Cat-cow — 8–10
  • World’s greatest stretch — 3 per side
  • 90/90 hip switches — 8 total
  • Hamstring stretch rocks — 8 per leg
  • Calf stretch against wall — 3 deep breaths per leg
  • Thoracic rotations — 6 per side
  • Child’s pose reach-throughs — 5 per side

Gap-Filler Block 2 Easy Rounds

  • Glute bridges — 12
  • Dead bugs — 8 per side
  • Bird dogs — 8 per side
  • Prone Y-T-W raises — 5 each position
  • Shoulder-blade squeezes or scapular pull-up hangs — 6–8

Easy Cool-Down

  • Kneeling hip-flexor stretch — 3 deep breaths per side
  • Figure-4 glute stretch — 3 deep breaths per side
  • Chest doorway stretch — 3 deep breaths per side
  • Lat stretch or child’s pose — 5 deep breaths

Weekly Layout Overview

  • Monday: Bodyweight + jump rope circuit
  • Tuesday: Rest / easy movement
  • Wednesday: Full body + pulling + curls
  • Thursday: Rest / recovery
  • Friday: Jump rope conditioning
  • Saturday or Sunday: Flexible stretching / gap-filler day
  • Other weekend day: Rest
